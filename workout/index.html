WorkOut Zone

Burpees
It is a very high intensity fullbody workout.For a beginner 6 reps is acceptable and slowly increase the number of reps do the exercise slowly and carefully.
Alternate Forward Lunges
It is a exercise for the thighs and hip flexors focus on one leg at a time and try to do atleast 20 reps on each leg.
Back Extensions
It is a very strict exercise that focuses on your spine for some grip use a towel and drag it to extend your back Try to do atleast 30 reps.
Bent Down Touches
It is a active rest that focus on hamstrings and spine and gives some relief.Try to do 30 reps.
Cat Cow Pose
It is a active rest that focus on glutes,hip flexors and spine and gives some relief.Try to do atleast 30 reps.
Cobra Pose
It is a active rest that focus on hip flexors and spine and gives some relief.Try to do atleast 30 reps.
Cross Scissors
Its is a exercise that focuses on the core try to do this exercise carfelly and try to do atleast 30 reps totally.
Crunches Plus
This is abdominal crunches and it is a exercise that focuses on the whole ab area try to do slowly and try to do atleast 20 reps.
Dead Bugs
It is a active rest that focus on stability,glutes,hip flexors and spine and gives some relief.Try to do atleast 30 reps.
High Knees
It is a running motion with a high jump it is a great cardio vascular movement and keeping your hands behind your head ups the intensity.Try to do for atleast 30 seconds. and it also focuses on the core.
Jumping Jacks
It is a full body cardio vascular movement that uses all the parts in the body and is a great exerice to warm up the body.Try to do for atleast 30 seconds.
Plank Lifts
It is a exercise that focuses on the core and stabilty and overall body management it is static exercise.Try to do atleast for 30 seconds.
Push Ups
It is a classic exercise that is used to pump the chest and improve the hands and shoulders.If you are not able to normal pushups try by putting your knee on the ground and do the motion.Try to do atleast 10 reps.
Side Skaters
It is a full body cardio vascular movement that uses all the parts in the body and is a great exerice to body.Try to do for atleast 30 seconds.
